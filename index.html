Lacona 2 for Mac

Don't just search. Command.

Open Lacona with a keystroke and start typing. Find your apps and files instantly.

Previews

But don't just search—run powerful commands using plain English to speed up your most common tasks.

Nothing to configure, nothing to memorize. Just get stuff done.

Features

  • Launch and activate apps
  • Search and open system preferences
  • Search, navigate, open, and show files
  • Move, duplicate, and delete files
  • Eject drives
  • Open URLs
  • Perform web searches
  • Math, unit conversions, currency conversions, time zone math, and more (powered by Soulver)
  • Search and open contacts
  • Search, open, and create, and delete calendar events
  • Search, open, and create, and complete reminders
  • Search and open reminder lists
  • Open Calendar to particular dates
  • Make calls (FaceTime or iPhone)
  • Send messages (iMessage or SMS)
  • Send emails
